is an android widget/application which displays the current values of the weatherstation mythenquai or tiefenbrunnen, which is provided by the water city police of zurich. To reduce data transfer the data is provided through appengine from google.
Following actions can be performed:
  • year and month charts, sliding between charts, zurich weatherstation selection possible with preferences: mythenquai or tiefenbrunnen (v1.05)
  • daycharts, bug fix widget startup (v1.04)
  • switched to the weatherstation tiefenbrunnen because mythenquai is out of service till mid december (v1.03)
  • help menu, app localized in english and german (v1.02)
  • a widget display the current air temperature, humidity and lake zurich water temperature, it is updated every half hour (v1.01)
  • the application displays all current weather values and displays them in a list view (v1.0)
